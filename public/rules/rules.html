CryptHunt Rules

Please read and follow the rules for an exciting and fair competition.

  • Everything you see on your screen may or may not be related to the final answer.

  • Each team can have up to 2 participants, and only one team per school is allowed.

  • No team is allowed to help or contact any other participating team regarding the hunt.

  • Answers must be written in all small caps without spaces. Example: "mynameishenry".

  • Only letters and numbers are allowed in the answers.

  • Participants must log in with the provided username and password to access the hunt.

  • Unnecessary pings or spam will result in a 1-hour delay in receiving leads.

  • Teams are not allowed to ask for leads via DMs. A dedicated LEADS channel will be provided for all lead requests.

  • If you are using a mobile device, enable 'Desktop site' in your browser for a better experience.

  • Regular Hints will be posted on our discord server.

Have a great time hunting!
